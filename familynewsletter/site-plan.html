Overview

Purpose

Family is the most important thing, but we don’t always have the ability to meet up. While we wait to see each other, our newsletter will share updates, photos, and milestones so loved ones can feel connected. It’s a simple way to keep grandparents, aunts, uncles, cousins, and friends in the loop.

Audience

Grandparents, aunts, uncles, cousins, friends, and all we love and want to share updates with.
