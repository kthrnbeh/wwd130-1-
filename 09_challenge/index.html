Climbing a mountain isn’t just about reaching the top—it’s about discovering what we’re capable of along the way. When we decide to take on a mountain, we’re not only challenging our bodies, but also our patience, courage, and determination. Each step upward teaches us something: how to push through when the path gets steep, how to appreciate small victories, and how to find strength in teamwork.
